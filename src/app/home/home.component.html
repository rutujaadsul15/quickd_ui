As a sender, you can easily schedule a parcel for delivery through our user-friendly website. But here's the exciting twist ‚Äì anyone heading in the direction of your parcel's destination can become a carrier and earn by delivering it for you! Imagine turning your everyday commute into an opportunity to make a difference and earn extra cash.

To begin, log in to pickmyparcel.in. Click on "Send New Parcel", enter your parcel details, make the payment & be ready with your parcel.

Our authorized carrier, who will be traveling to the desired destination of your parcel, will get in touch with you and collect your package.

Once you have handed over your parcel to the carrier, simply lock the deal on our portal by selecting deal under "My Submitted Parcels" menu.

The carrier will safely transport your parcel to its intended destination.

Once reached at the desired location, carrier will contact receiver regarding parcel collection.

The carrier will hand over the parcel to the receiver after validating the OTP that was sent to the receiver at the time of booking.
